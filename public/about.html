Just exactly what is NoseGoes?

We are the solution to everyday assignements in your party! We help you assign tasks to anyone in your group in an orderly fashion.

We believe in recognizing greatness, and if you finish your tasks in your group you should be rewarded. But we also shame those who don't, becuase frankly who would want to be that person?

Finish your tasks, and you will get points. In your group, if you have the least amount of points, you will recieve a consequences based on how the group determines it. It may be more tasks in the folowign wek, pay the highest points group member, or being able to draw and humiliate you in public. Good Luck!
